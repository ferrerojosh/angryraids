What is AngryRaids?

AngryRaids is an equipment simulator for King's Raid.

Why do we need this?

This is for people who are gung-ho about their builds and wanting to see how it will turn out before farming them.

Isn't excel good enough for this?

Yes, but there are people who can't use excel in a good way.

Why isn't there enough information about each hero?

This thing solves equipment simulation, for the lack of hero information, there is MaskOfGoblin for that.

Feel free to open a pull request on GitHub.
